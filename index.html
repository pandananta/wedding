Hotels

We recommend staying in Santa Monica for convenient access to the wedding events (15-30 minute drive). The typical hotel room near the Santa Monica pier / beach ranges between $300 and $400 per night. You may find more economical options on AirBnB, or in neighborhoods that are farther away from the wedding such as: Agoura Hills, Marina Del Rey or near LAX. Please note that LA traffic can be intense, especially along the Pacific Coast Highway. You'll want to plan a larger travel buffer the farther away you stay. Feel free to to contact us if you want specific hotel or roommate suggestions.

Transportation

Cars are necessary for getting around in LA. We recommend renting a car, or using the Uber ride-hailing app, which is much cheaper than taxis in Los Angeles. We also urge everyone to carpool as much as possible, as parking can be scarce by the Malibu Pier.
