Hotels

Our wedding is a tiny affair, so we haven't reserved any room blocks. We recommend staying in Santa Monica or Agoura Hills for convenient access to the wedding events. You may also find decent hotel options in Marina Del Rey or near LAX. Please note that LA traffic can be intense, especially along the Pacific Coast Highway. You'll want to plan a larger travel buffer the farther away you stay.

Transportation

Cars are necessary for getting around in LA. We recommend renting a car, or using the Uber ride-hailing app, which is much cheaper than taxis in Los Angeles. Fare estimates for Uber are available here.

Parking

Please note: you will NOT be able to leave your car at Lake Shrine after the ceremony, as the lot will be needed for another wedding. We highly encourage carpooling, since parking can be scarce at both locations. Please be prepared pay to $10-$15 for parking near the pier.
